An employee who comes to your team via an acquisition<\/p>\n\n\n\n

  • was likely in the dark until the acquisition was announced<\/li>
  • didn’t apply to be part of your company<\/span>, your team, or for their new job<\/li>
  • is accustomed to working on different things, with different people, toward different goals<\/span><\/li>
  • doesn’t know you, or anyone on your team, or your company<\/li>
  • is unaware of your expectations of them<\/li>
  • experiences a loss of the status<\/span> they enjoyed as a valued employee prior to the acquisition<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n
