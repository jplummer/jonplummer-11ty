Gentle people,<\/p>\n

It’s a moment of uncertainty and anxiety (election), piled atop a lengthy period of uncertainty and anxiety (pandemic, police killings, climate change, fires, etc.) with no real end in sight. I get it if you’re distracted. It’s fine\u2014expected, even.<\/p>\n

Don’t want to talk about it? Fine. Want to talk about it? Fine. I’m here for that, either way.<\/p>\n
