> What do you do when an executive brings you a prototype they vibe-coded they are super-excited about?

Note that “let’s talk about your aims behind this, what it’s meant to accomplish, how it does that” is the appropriate response to ANYONE’s mock/prototype/sketch and we’re going to need to be real good at that since roles are blurring and tools are helping folks make stuff they might not have bothered with before. And especially really good at offering alternatives to simplify, make more conformant with customer mental models, detect and form a plan to learn things we need to learn, etc.
